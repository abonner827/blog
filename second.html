This is my second most recent article on positive affirmations of the day. You have a choice each and every single Day. I choose to feel Blessed, Grateful, Excited, Thankful and Happy. TEN POSITIVE AFFIRMATIONS TO BOOST YOUR MOOD EVERY DAY Here is our list of positive affirmations! Choose your favorite and speak it out to start your day. PERSONALIZE by adding more statements that are specific to your situation. I also added some tips to help. :1. I AM BEAUTIFUL. You can’t control what people think about you, but you can choose to see the good in you. Speak out and say what you like about you. Take good care of yourself and enhance your beauty. 2. I AM ACCEPTED. Be the first to accept who you are, flaws and all. Forgive yourself for not being perfect, because nobody is. When you learn to embrace yourself, you make it easier for others to see who you really are and love you for it. 3. I AM VALUABLE. You are important and you bring value to this world. There are ideas that only you can think of, there are people that only you can reach. A kind word from you can change someone else’s life. 4. I AM LOVED. Be the first to love yourself. Be thankful for each person who added value to your life. Imagine those whom you will meet in the future and build happy relationships with. Attract love. 5. I HAVE A POSITIVE ATTITUDE IN ANY SITUATION. You are in control of your reactions and you can choose to be positive. The more you practice this, the more you are able to make good decisions in times of crisis. 6. I KEEP ON LEARNING NEW THINGS. There is no limit to what you can do, as long as you keep on learning. View difficulties and challenges as opportunities to learn new things. 7. I ALWAYS HOPE FOR THE BEST IN MY LIFE. Always believe that there are better things ahead of you. Though failures and disappointments are painful, they also teach valuable lessons in life. Learn from them and keep moving forward. 8. I ATTRACT GOOD THINGS IN LIFE. Keep on doing good and good will keep on coming back to you. Expect good to happen to you every day and receive it with a thankful heart. 9. I WAS MADE FOR A PURPOSE. You were born in this day and age for a reason that no one else can fulfill. Your choices shape your world and those around you, so keep making good choices. 10. I WAS BORN FOR GREATNESS. Greatness comes to those who are willing to serve, so take the opportunity to help others. You can be great in the eyes of the world or in the heart of a child.
